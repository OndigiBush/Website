Logbook Loan

Swift Capital offers quick loans against Logbooks. Using our service, you can borrow as much as 60% of the value of your car within three hours and repay it within 18 months.

Asset Financing

Have you always wanted a new car but can’t afford the hefty price tag? Asset financing addresses your needs by helping you own the luxury car of your dreams. You don’t have to put a huge down payment down. We Finance up to 60% the value of the car

Investment Plan

Oliver Wendell Holmes says “Put not your trust in money, but your money in trust.” That’s why we offer you an opportunity to partner with us. Partnering with us means you’ll make more money. You’ll also receive unlimited access to our platform.

Import Financing

We will finance up to 60% of the total estimate cost (CIF, Duty & Port Charges) of Importing your vehicle while handling the Importation process and clearance at the Port.

About Us

Swift Capital Limited is a microfinance company in Nairobi that provides working capital solutions to both individuals and Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). We are a non-deposit taking micro-finance company that offers tailor-made financial solutions to meet cash flow challenges faced by its target clientele, both individuals and SMEs. We are a customer focused relationship based micro-finance company with a range of loan products custom-made to suit the borrower’s needs. Our competitive edge is offering a wide range of financial solutions, timely and at competitive pricing
